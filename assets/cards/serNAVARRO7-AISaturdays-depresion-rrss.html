Social Media and Depression Symptoms

This Data Package contains the raw data for the following publication: Aalbers, G., McNally, R. J., Heeren, A., De Wit, S., & Fried, E. I. (2019). Social media and depression symptoms: A network perspective. Journal of Experimental Psychology: General, 148(8), 1454. http://dx.doi.org/10.1037/xge0000528 The raw data can be found at the Open Science Framework: https://osf.io/czs6y/
